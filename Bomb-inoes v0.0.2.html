How to Play

OBJECTIVE

A 5-round domino match against the AI. Win 3 rounds to take the match. The puzzle is seeded daily — everyone plays the same draw each day. Your match carries over if you close mid-game.

PLAYING
• The player with the highest double goes first. If neither holds a double, the highest pip total leads.
• Tap a tile in your hand to select it, then tap an open end of the chain that it matches.
• The chain grows from both ends — each end has a pip value that the next tile must match.
BONEYARD
• If you can't play, tap DRAW to take one tile from the boneyard.
• If the drawn tile fits you may play it immediately; if not, your turn passes. Each draw costs you points.
SCORING
• Empty hand win: 100 + opponent's remaining pips − (your draws × 10), min 100.
• Blocked game (nobody can play): 50 + pip differential − (your draws × 10), min 50.
• Win 3 of 5 rounds to claim the match. Your total match score is saved to the leaderboard.
